The Future Diary
Season 1 Episode 1 HD SUB
The Future Diary is a survival battle royale anime and manga where twelve individuals are given "Future Diaries"—special devices that predict the future—by the God of Time and Space, Deus Ex Machina. The plot follows middle schooler Yukiteru Amano as he is thrust into this deadly game alongside his stalker, Yuno Gasai, to battle other contestants until only one remains to become the next God of Time and Space. The series is known for its dark themes of blood, suspense, and psychological twists, as the characters must use their diaries to kill each other to survive.
