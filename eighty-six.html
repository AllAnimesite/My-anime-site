86 (Eighty Six) is an anime about the Republic of San Magnolia, which forces the persecuted "86" to pilot mechs in a war falsely claimed to be fought by unmanned drones. It follows Lena, an idealistic handler, and Shin, the 86’s battle-hardened leader, as she discovers the harsh truth behind the so-called "bloodless" war.
