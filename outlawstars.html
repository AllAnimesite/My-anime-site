Outlaw Stars is a space adventure anime about Gene Starwind and his partner Jim Hawking, who stumble upon the mysterious ship Outlaw Star. Together with their quirky crew—including an assassin, a bio-android, and a catgirl—they travel across the galaxy in search of treasure, freedom, and the legendary Galactic Leyline, while battling pirates, assassins, and rival outlaws.
