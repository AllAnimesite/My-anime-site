Season 1 Episode 1 HD SUB
Hajime no Ippo: New Challenger is the second anime season continuing Ippo Makunouchi's journey after becoming the Japanese Featherweight Champion, as he faces new challenges, defends his title, and learns about the world of boxing. It focuses on Ippo's goal to reach the world championship, encountering both familiar rivals like Miyata and new formidable opponents, while the series continues to explore themes of perseverance, discipline, and character development in the realistic world of boxing.
